# Strategic Review

Defines how leadership reviews strategy, performance, market changes, execution gaps, customer signals, financial progress, and operating adjustments. This is where [BUSINESS-OPERATING-SYSTEM.md](BUSINESS-OPERATING-SYSTEM.md) principle 1 — "strategy follows evidence" — actually happens on a schedule.

## What Gets Reviewed

| Area | Evidence source |
|------|-------------------|
| Strategy fit | Is [COMPANY-STRATEGY.md](COMPANY-STRATEGY.md) still the right direction given what's been learned |
| Performance | [BUSINESS-METRICS.md](BUSINESS-METRICS.md), and progress against [GOALS-AND-OKRS.md](GOALS-AND-OKRS.md) |
| Market changes | Updated evidence against [MARKET-AND-CUSTOMER-STRATEGY.md](MARKET-AND-CUSTOMER-STRATEGY.md)'s assumptions |
| Execution gaps | Where goals were missed, and why — not just that they were missed |
| Customer signals | [CUSTOMER-FEEDBACK-LOOP.md](../09-operations/CUSTOMER-FEEDBACK-LOOP.md), [CUSTOMER-HEALTH.md](../13-customer-success/CUSTOMER-HEALTH.md) themes |
| Financial progress | Revenue, margin, runway against [FINANCIAL-MODEL-AND-ASSUMPTIONS.md](../08-investors/FINANCIAL-MODEL-AND-ASSUMPTIONS.md) |
| Operating risk | [BUSINESS-RISKS-AND-ASSUMPTIONS.md](BUSINESS-RISKS-AND-ASSUMPTIONS.md), including domain-level risk (technical debt, security trends, churn) |

## Conducting a Review

State what was expected, what actually happened, and why — a review that only reports numbers without examining the gap between expectation and reality isn't a strategic review, it's a status update.

## Operating Adjustments

A review that finds a real gap produces a specific adjustment — a changed priority, a revised goal, a new risk mitigation, an updated assumption — not just a discussion. A review with no resulting change either found nothing material or isn't being taken seriously.

## Cadence

Runs on [OPERATING-CADENCE.md](OPERATING-CADENCE.md)'s rhythm — typically aligned with monthly/quarterly reviews, with a deeper version at annual planning.

## Required Artifacts

A review record per cycle: what was checked, what was found, what's changing as a result.

## Ownership

Leadership conducts and owns strategic review; domain owners supply the evidence from their area and are accountable for explaining execution gaps in their own domain.

## Anti-Patterns

- **Review as reporting.** Presenting metrics without examining whether they confirm or contradict the strategy.
- **No resulting change.** A review that identifies a real gap but changes nothing afterward.
- **Reviewing only good news.** Skipping or softening the discussion of a missed goal or a growing risk.
